Mental Health





For the following questions, please answer to the best of your ability!

  • 1. How are you feeling today?



  • 2. How stressed are you on a scale of 1-10? (10-very stressed)



  • 3. Do you feel supported by your family and friends?



  • 4. Have you noticed any difficulties in sleeping or getting enough rest over the past few months?

    • Examples
      1. Having a difficult time falling asleep.
      2. Losing focus and feeling sleepy throughout the day.
      3. Always feeling tired.


  • 5. What are your hopes and dreams for the future?



  • 6. Do you know where to find professional help or guidance for mental health?
